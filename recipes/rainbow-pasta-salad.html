Rainbow Pasta Salad

Description

All you'll need are five ingredients to create this bright, beautifully flavored salad. Juicy diced tomatoes and assertive red onion blend perfectly in this Italian inspired dish that will have guest raving. This is a really quick and easy pasta salad. Great for picnics and BBQ's

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounces) package of tri-colored pasta assortment
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (16 ounces) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Bring a large pit of lightly salted water to boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, drain and rinse in cold water
  3. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, cooled pasta and Italian dressing. Mix well.
  4. Regrigerate overnight, or for at least 1 hour
